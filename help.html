What is Memory Lane?

MemoryLane™ is an interactive photo album that rekindles past memories and sparks conversations with anyone else it is shared with. It collects the users photos and uses object and facial recognition to sort the photos into categories. MemoryLane™ then allows you to select a photo or category and will automatically generate a short photo album (Photo Lane) with similar photos and conversational prompts for you to answer with whoever you’re sharing it with!
